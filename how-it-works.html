How it works: a replayable measurement pipeline for structural support in AI reasoning

Reflective Diagnostics evaluates whether an AI system’s conclusions are structurally supported by its stated premises. It does not score outputs for quality or correctness. It measures the internal support structure and produces a replayable evidence record suitable for audit and external review.

Each stage produces a fixed artifact that becomes the input to the next. No hidden rewrites. No silent retries. No adaptive steering.

Controlled Run

Ensures differences are attributable to the model, not the setup.

  • Same input and constraints across models
  • Model identity and version recorded
  • No retries, no prompt rewriting

Artifact Capture

Preserves outputs exactly as produced.

  • Raw model outputs stored verbatim
  • Run metadata captured (timestamps, IDs, selection)
  • Hashes generated to prevent silent alteration

Structural Extraction

Transforms narrative output into an inspectable support structure.

  • Claims, premises, and conclusions explicitly identified
  • Support relationships made visible
  • Orphaned or unsupported conclusions exposed

Signal Computation

Quantifies integrity from the extracted structure.

  • Coverage
  • Orphan rate
  • Stability
  • Structural integrity

Interpreter

Surfaces agreement, divergence, and failure modes across models.

  • Cross-model synthesis under identical constraints
  • Divergence and consistency made explicit
  • Failure modes linked to supporting artifacts

Evidence Packet

Produces a replayable, audit-grade case file.

  • Replayable record of inputs, outputs, and signals
  • Exportable forensic exhibit format
  • Designed for audit, governance, and funding review
