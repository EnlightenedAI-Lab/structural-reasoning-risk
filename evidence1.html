Proof & Evidence

The following exhibits demonstrate a working, end-to-end instrument. Each image corresponds to a fixed stage in the evaluation pipeline and exists to establish that the system is real, controlled, and auditable.

01 · Controlled Probe Setup

Identical prompt, explicit model selection, temperature control, and evidence capture enabled prior to execution.

02 · Cross-Model Arena Output

Multiple models responding under identical constraints, exposing structural differences rather than stylistic ones.

03 · Evidence Locker

Each run is persisted with a unique case ID, enabling replay, inspection, and audit after the fact.

04 · Raw Forensic Artifact

Unmodified model output and metadata captured verbatim to ensure provenance and reproducibility.

05 · L2 Executive Summary

Quantified structural metrics (integrity, coverage, orphans, stability) computed from extracted reasoning structure.

06 · Trace X-Ray

Claim-premise-conclusion mapping showing exactly where support exists or breaks down.

07 · Structural Telemetry

Deep structural signals used to detect hallucination risk, overconfidence, and fragility.

08 · Density Profile

Comparative structural geometry illustrating how models differ in support density and vulnerability.

09 · Risk Diagnosis

Automated detection of structural vulnerability with recommended escalation and export options.

10 · Interpreter Synthesis

Cross-model consensus and ranking, converting raw metrics into decision-grade interpretation.
