Disclosure & Scope
  • This is a demonstration of an assurance workflow on a publicly available document.
  • It does not evaluate the organization or the proposal quality.
  • It checks structural readiness only: claim extraction, evidence markers, and governance indicators.
  • “CUBICS Assurance” is a working title for the demo; not an official Cubics tool.
  • Outputs are generated from a deterministic script; analyst review can override.

CUBICS Assurance

Independent assurance gate for Cubics submissions: validates evidence, accountability, and audit trail readiness before Council review.

CUBICS Assurance does not score ideas or technologies; it validates whether submitted claims are sufficiently evidenced, owned, and auditable to proceed.

  • Ensures claims made to investors are structurally tied to evidence.
  • Automates the detection of governance gaps and missing accountability trails.
  • Provides an independent, auditable artifact for compliance records.

Assurance Modules

Module A

Claim–Evidence Consistency

Scans document submissions to map every affirmative claim to its supporting premise. Detects unsupported assertions and logical gaps.

Module B

Governance & Accountability

Validates the presence of required audit trails, accountable owners, and verifiable timestamps for key decisions.

Module C

Risk & Integrity Signals

Analyzes semantic patterns to detect overconfidence, "marketing fluff" disguised as fact, and missing substantiation.

Assurance Signals

Signal Meaning What failure looks like
structural_coherence Degree to which conclusions logically follow from stated premises. Conclusions that "sound right" but lack evidentiary steps.
premise_coverage Percentage of distinct claims supported by at least one piece of evidence. A deck full of assertions with no reference citations.
orphan_rate Volume of claims that have zero structural support links. "We will dominate the market" (orphaned from market data).
evidence_gap_risk Composite probability that a statement is fabricated or lacks source documents. Specific numbers or dates that appear without source documents.
overconfidence_gap Distance between the certainty of language and the strength of evidence. Using words like "guaranteed" or "proven" on weak premises.
claim_stability Consistency of the claim meaning under stress-testing. Claims that change meaning when phrased slightly differently.

Compliance

Time

Cost

Minimizes billable hours spent on basic structural assurance cycles.

Risk

System Workflow

System Inputs
  • KID PDF / Word Documents
  • Supporting Annexes (Market study, Capex sheets)
  • Engineering Notes & Technical Claims
  • Claims Register (Auto-generated or Manual)
Assurance Outputs
  • 1-Page Assurance Summary (PASS/WARN/FAIL)
  • Claim–Evidence Map (CSV + Viewer)
  • Governance Gap List (Missing Owners)
  • Audit Log Bundle (Hashes + Timestamps)
Assurance Status is non-binding but auditable; final approval remains with the Council of Sages.

Detected Red Flags

  • Critical: Section 4.2 contains 3 revenue projections with zero linked evidence.
  • Governance: "Technical Feasibility" section lacks a named accountable owner.
  • Integrity: Conclusion on "Market Dominance" does not logically follow from the provided premises.
